Quick Registration

Tip: Your browser will suggest saved details. If you don't see suggestions, see the notes below.

If autofill doesn't show

  1. Make sure you're opening this page over HTTPS on your site (not from file://).
  2. Chrome: Settings → Autofill → Addresses and more → turn on & add your name, phone, and email.
  3. Safari (iPhone/Mac): Settings → Safari → AutoFill → Use Contact Info ON, and your contact card has name/phone/email.
  4. Click/tap inside each field once; browsers only suggest after focus and user interaction.
